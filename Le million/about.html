Where It All Began

Le Million was founded in 2024 in the heart of Paris — in a small atelier tucked between centuries-old boulevards and whispered secrets. Our founder, a former gemologist with 20 years of experience across the world's most prestigious maisons, wanted to create something different.

Not jewelry made for display cases. Jewelry made to be worn, to age with you, to carry your story. Every stone is selected in person. Every setting is hand-finished. Every piece is numbered and certified by our master artisans.

We don't chase trends. We build legacies.

The Art of Selection

Our gemologists travel to Colombia for emeralds, to Myanmar for rubies, to Kashmir for sapphires. We source only from ethical, certified mines — because beauty should never come at a cost to people or planet.

Every diamond in a Le Million piece is GIA-certified. Every gold alloy is responsibly sourced and tested in-house. The entire process — from rough stone to finished jewel — takes an average of 8 weeks.

This is not mass production. This is craft.
